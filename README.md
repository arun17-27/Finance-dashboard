# Finance-dashboard
This project presents a specialized Finance Dashboard developed in Power BI to analyze organizational financial health and operational efficiency. It serves as a practical application of financial modeling and data visualization, designed to provide stakeholders with a clear view of revenue streams, profitability, and cost management

Key Financial Insights & Features

Revenue & Profitability Analysis: Comprehensive tracking of gross profit, net margins, and revenue growth trends over time.  
Advanced DAX Measures: Implementation of complex DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for financial calculations, including Year-over-Year (YoY) growth and fiscal period comparisons.  
Expense Monitoring: Detailed breakdown of operational costs to identify potential areas for optimization and budget adherence.  
Interactive Financial Modeling: Dynamic slicers allow for granular analysis by fiscal quarter, region, or product line, providing a multi-dimensional view of financial data.

Technical Skills Demonstrated

Data Transformation: Utilizing Power Query to clean and structure raw financial data for accurate reporting.  
Relational Data Modeling: Designing an efficient schema to handle complex financial datasets while maintaining high report performance.  
Executive Visualization: Designing high-impact visuals and KPI cards that translate technical data into actionable business intelligence.

Tools & Technologies

Power BI Desktop: Core platform for report design and modeling.  
DAX: Used for advanced financial logic and custom measure creation.  
Financial Datasets: Structured data focusing on profit and loss (P&L) and balance sheet metrics.
